SFA - Structure based Fingerprint Alignment Tool


SFA reads two input sequences and writes their optimal global sequence alignment. We introduce a structure-based fingerprint scoring method as a key component of our alignment approach. This method leverages the structural features of proteins to assign scores to alignments, giving higher weights to matches between structurally similar residues.
